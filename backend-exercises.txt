In the backend we need a new endpoint called: exercises 

Data for excersises are in two different categories: cardio & strength. 

A cardio record will have following parameters (demo data, simply to be able to extract datatype):

{"type": "cardio",
"id": autoincrement,
"name": "Name of excercise",
"minutes": 30,
"level": 10,
"steps": 100,
"distance": 10.8,
"": 0,
"calories": 400}

A strength record will have following parameters:

{"type": "weight",
"id": autoincrement,
"name": "Name of excercise",
"sets": 3,
"repetitions": 10,
"weight": 40.5,
"calories": 400}

Unique field are "id" (which may be the mongodb _id) and "name"
